Wild Bill Shakespeare

An English IV Webquest

Directions:

Write the answers to the following questions down and keep them for quizzes and tests.

2. What was his father's name?
3. What was his father's occupation?
4. What political office did his father hold?
5. What was his mother's name?
6. When and to whom did Shakespeare marry?
7. How many children did they have?
8. Why is the year 1592 important when learning about Shakespeare's life?
9. Who were the Lord Chamberlain's men?
10. How many plays did Shakespeare write?
11. When and where did Shakespeare retire?
12. When did he die? Why is that ironic?
13. Where is he buried?
14. Read the verse carved in stone above his grave. What do those lines mean?
15. On what document do we find one of the few samples of Shakespeare's handwriting that we can still view today?
