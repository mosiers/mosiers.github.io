Welcome to Smyrna High School!

Greetings and salutations, my name is Mr. Mosier, and I teach English I and IV here at SHS. If you ever need to reach me, please feel free to email me at mosiers@rcs.k12.tn.us. I am looking forward to another exciting year discussing End of Course objectives, short stories, Homer's The Odyssey, Shakespeare, Lord of the Flies, and various poems. The focus of the literature in English I is the idea of the journey. As we move forward, we will experience many journeys through the eyes of the characters we encounter within the stories we read and discuss. The focus in English IV is on British literature and life skills. As we move forward, we will find correlations between the selections we read and the skills we need to succeed in the world.
